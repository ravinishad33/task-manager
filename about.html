User Guide

Master your productivity with these simple steps.

1

Capture Tasks

Type your task in the input field and hit Enter or click Add. Your tasks are saved instantly.

2

Track Progress

Click on any task to mark it as complete. The progress bar at the top will update in real-time as you crush your goals.

3

Zero Data Loss

We use LocalStorage technology. This means your tasks stay on your computer even if you close the tab or restart your browser.
